About Juleps Home Decor

About Juleps

Juleps' Accents & Beyond is a full service design studio for Interior Design that will define your lifestyle. Sumaya Maghariaf, owner of Juleps, graduated from the Art Institute of Atlanta with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Interior Design. She worked with some of Atlanta's fines design firms before opening her own residential commercial design practice-- design known as sophisticated, eclectic sense of style.

The showroom located in West Cobb showcases an array of furniture, custom draperies, framed art, lighting, mirrors, accessories, and much more.

Seasonal Decorating

This holiday season, simplify your life by taking decorating your home off your mind. Our experienced design team will create the perfect setting for your holiday entertainment and celebration. Choose from our wide selection of completely decorated garlands, unique ornaments, custom designed trees, wreaths, and arrangements. We work within your budget to achieve the look you desire.

Interior Decorating Services

Juleps provides a full design service and in-home consultations to suggest colors, fabrics, and furnishings. Whether it's a new construction or remodeling, our designers can help achieve the look you're after.

Work scales vary from coordinating granite samples with floor finishes to dressing up walls and accessorizing. We are able to order both furniture and accessories from catalogs as well as draw from our retail showroom located in Marietta.

Our floral designers can put together simple arrangments to large floral designs, whether it's seasonal or year round.


Window Treatments

Creating a beautiful window treatment that meets the specifications of the project is all about making the right choices. Juleps provides a large variety of fabric, heading, and puts the finishing touches that personalizes each project with emblishments.
